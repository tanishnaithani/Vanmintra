About FRA Atlas & Our Team

Problem Statement

Development of AI-powered FRA Atlas and WebGIS-based Decision Support System (DSS) for Integrated Monitoring of Forest Rights Act (FRA) Implementation across Madhya Pradesh, Tripura, Odisha, and Telangana.

Our Approach

We combine AI analytics with interactive WebGIS mapping to provide real-time monitoring, transparent claim tracking, and data-driven insights for FRA implementation. Our system enables stakeholders to visualize FRA progress, identify bottlenecks, and make informed decisions.

Forest Rights Act (FRA) 2006

The Forest Rights Act (FRA) was enacted in 2006 to recognize and vest forest rights in Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers who have been residing in forests for generations. The act aims to correct historical injustices faced by forest-dwelling communities while ensuring conservation of forests.

Key provisions include recognition of individual forest rights, community forest rights, and habitat rights for Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs).

VanMitra Integration

VanMitra is our proof-of-concept platform that demonstrates the integration of AI and GIS technologies for forest governance. It serves as the foundation for FRA Atlas, providing:

  • Real-time forest monitoring capabilities
  • Community engagement tools
  • Data collection and validation mechanisms
  • Multi-stakeholder collaboration features

VanMitra's success in pilot regions has informed the development of FRA Atlas, ensuring practical applicability and user-centered design.
